Uso del Presente Simple

Oraciones Afirmativas

Las oraciones afirmativas en presente simple se utilizan para expresar hechos generales, hábitos o acciones que se repiten en el tiempo. Para formar estas oraciones, se usa la forma base del verbo con todos los sujetos excepto en tercera persona singular (he, she, it), donde se añade una "-s" al verbo.

Ejemplos:

  • I walk to school. (Yo camino a la escuela.)
  • He walks to school. (Él camina a la escuela.)

Ejercicio 1: She (to read) books.

She reads books.

Ejercicio 2: They (to play) soccer.

They play soccer.

Ejercicio 3: He (to work) at a bank.

He works at a bank.

Ejercicio 4: We (to study) English.

We study English.

Ejercicio 5: She (to live) in Madrid.

She lives in Madrid.

Ejercicio 6: They (to have) a dog.

They have a dog.

Ejercicio 7: I (to eat) breakfast.

I eat breakfast.

Ejercicio 8: You (to play) the guitar.

You play the guitar.

Ejercicio 9: She (to visit) her grandparents.

She visits her grandparents.

Ejercicio 10: We (to travel) to the beach.

We travel to the beach.

Oraciones Negativas

Las oraciones negativas en presente simple se forman usando "do not" (don't) o "does not" (doesn't) seguido de la forma base del verbo. Usamos "do not" con los sujetos I, you, we, they y "does not" con los sujetos he, she, it.

Ejemplos:

  • I do not (don't) like ice cream. (No me gusta el helado.)
  • She does not (doesn't) like ice cream. (A ella no le gusta el helado.)

Ejercicio 1: They (to like) ice cream.

They do not like ice cream.

Ejercicio 2: He (to have) a car.

He does not have a car.

Ejercicio 3: She (to eat) vegetables.

She does not eat vegetables.

Ejercicio 4: We (to like) the movie.

We do not like the movie.

Ejercicio 5: I (to know) the answer.

I do not know the answer.

Ejercicio 6: They (to work) on weekends.

They do not work on weekends.

Ejercicio 7: She (to drive) to work.

She does not drive to work.

Ejercicio 8: We (to visit) the museum.

We do not visit the museum.

Ejercicio 9: He (to play) basketball.

He does not play basketball.

Ejercicio 10: You (to sing) in the choir.

You do not sing in the choir.

Oraciones Interrogativas

Para formular preguntas en presente simple, usamos "do" o "does" al principio de la oración seguido del sujeto y la forma base del verbo. Usamos "do" con I, you, we, they y "does" con he, she, it.

Ejemplos:

  • Do you play football? (¿Juegas al fútbol?)
  • Does he play football? (¿Él juega al fútbol?)

Ejercicio 1: (He/to play) football?

Does he play football?

Ejercicio 2: (They/to go) to the park?

Do they go to the park?

Ejercicio 3: (She/to speak) English?

Does she speak English?

Ejercicio 4: (We/to eat) lunch together?

Do we eat lunch together?

Ejercicio 5: (I/to have) a pet?

Do I have a pet?

Ejercicio 6: (They/to watch) TV?

Do they watch TV?

Ejercicio 7: (He/to cook) dinner?

Does he cook dinner?

Ejercicio 8: (You/to study) hard?

Do you study hard?

Ejercicio 9: (She/to work) from home?

Does she work from home?

Ejercicio 10: (We/to travel) by train?

Do we travel by train?
